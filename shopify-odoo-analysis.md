**Incumbent 1: Manual Spreadsheets**

- **What they do:** Track products in Excel, copy-paste between platforms  
- **Cost:** ₹0

- **Why it's sticky:** No switching cost \= they stay until pain is unbearable

**Incumbent 2: EasyEcom**

- **What they do:** Unified OMS (Order Management System) for Amazon, Flipkart, Meesho, Shopify  
- **Cost:** ₹2 per order processed  
- **Why people use it:** Real-time order sync, centralized dashboard  
- **Switching cost:** MEDIUM-HIGH (API integration, data migration, retraining)  
- **Why it's sticky:** Integrated into seller workflow; switching \= operational disruption

**Incumbent 3: Ginesys OMS**

- **What they do:** Enterprise multi-channel operations (inventory, orders, payments)  
- **Cost:** $15-150/month depending on plan  
- **Why people use it:** Comprehensive; good reporting  
- **Switching cost:** MEDIUM-HIGH (complex setup, enterprise mindset)  
- **Why it's sticky:** Trusted brand; switching risk is high

**Incumbent 4: OMSGuru**

- **What they do:** Lightweight OMS for Indian marketplaces  
- **Cost:** ₹2 per order (free to start)  
- **Why people use it:** Simple, affordable, India-focused  
- **Switching cost:** LOW-MEDIUM (lightweight integration)  
- **Why it's sticky:** Cheap; good enough for small sellers

**Incumbent 5: Synkron (Shopify App)**

- **What they do:** Real-time inventory sync across Amazon, Flipkart, Shopify  
- **Cost:** Not found (likely $10-30/month)  
- **Why people use it:** Native Shopify integration; solves overselling  
- **Switching cost:** LOW (just uninstall the app)  
- **Why it's sticky:** Simplicity; Shopify ecosystem native

**Incumbent 6: Shopify Magic (Built-in)**

- **What they do:** AI-generated product descriptions  
- **Cost:** FREE (built into Shopify)  
- **Why people use it:** Free, integrated, convenient  
- **Switching cost:** ZERO  
- **Why it's sticky:** No switching cost; everyone has it
